BOLT 6' Cable Lock - Enhanced Security for Jeep Owners

The BOLT 6' Cable Lock is designed specifically for 2018 and newer Jeep center cut keys. With no need for an extra key on your key ring, it simplifies your routine and keeps your vehicle protected in one go. The 1/4" black vinyl coated coiled cable offers durability and flexibility, ensuring it can handle different environments and situations without breaking.

Featuring an auto-return spring, the lock engages automatically when you remove the key, providing convenience and security in equal measure. Its stainless steel shutter and double ball bearing locking mechanism make it highly resistant to dirt, moisture, picking, and bumping. Whether you're parking in a public lot, at a campsite, or leaving your vehicle overnight, this lock delivers reliable protection without the hassle.

The compact and user-friendly design makes it easy to store, fitting perfectly into your pocket or glovebox. Backed by a lifetime warranty, you can trust this product to last and perform consistently. Ideal for Jeep owners seeking added security without the complexity of managing multiple keys, the BOLT 6' Cable Lock is a must-have for modern vehicle protection.
