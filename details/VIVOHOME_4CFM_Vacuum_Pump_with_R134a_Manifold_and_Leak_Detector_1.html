VIVOHOME Vacuum Pump - Precision Tool for HVAC & Air Conditioning Maintenance

Whether you're a professional technician or a DIY enthusiast, the VIVOHOME vacuum pump is designed to help you achieve accurate, efficient, and reliable results in your AC, refrigeration, or HVAC work.

Why Choose Our Vacuum Pump?

With a high-efficiency 4CFM capacity, this vacuum pump quickly and effectively removes moisture from your system, ensuring a complete evacuation before refrigerant refill. Its powerful performance is perfect for both small-scale repairs and larger installations.

The die-cast aluminum body ensures durability and strength, while the anti-slip rubber base provides stability during operation. This reliable design is built to last and withstand the rigors of regular use in various environments.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Efficient Evacuation: The 4CFM vacuum pump delivers rapid, thorough system evacuation for quick service and precise refrigerant charging.
  • Durable Construction: A lightweight die-cast aluminum housing with a secure anti-slip base ensures stability and long-term use.
  • Easy Management: Color-coded hoses (red for high-pressure, blue for low-pressure, yellow for filling) simplify system identification and reduce the risk of errors during installation or repair.
  • Extended Operation: A built-in cooling fan prevents overheating, allowing for longer use without performance degradation or downtime.
  • Complete Kit: Includes a manifold gauge set, vacuum oil, and a leak detector for comprehensive maintenance and hassle-free storage.

Ideal for Every Refrigerant System

Whether you're working on residential, commercial, or automotive AC systems, this vacuum pump is compatible with a wide range of refrigerants, including R134a, R12, R22, R502, and R410a. The included leak detector helps maintain system integrity and ensures your work meets industry standards.
